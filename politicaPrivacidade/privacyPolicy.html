Introduction

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Clockin.it. We are committed to protecting personal information and the right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices with regards to personal information, please contact us at policy@clockin.it.

This Privacy Policy describes how Clockin.it ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and makes accessible information in connection with your use of our Clockin.it Register mobile application (the "App"). This App is specifically designed for use by authorized personnel, such as HR Managers of our client companies ("Clients"), to register facial biometric data of their employees ("Employees") for use within the Clockin.it time tracking system.

This policy primarily addresses the data processed through the App by the HR Manager. The handling of the Employee biometric data registered via this App is also governed by the privacy policies applicable to the Clockin.it services used by the Employee (e.g., Clockin.it Single) and the Client's own privacy policies, as the Client is the data controller for their Employee's data.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide the App's registration functionalities and to manage the biometric data within the Clockin.it ecosystem:

  • Information Collected or Provided via the App:
    • Employee Photos: The core function of the App involves capturing or uploading photos of Employees. This requires in-app permission to access the device Camera or Photo Gallery.
    • Biometric Data: We process the uploaded Employee photos using biometric recognition analysis to extract the facial landmarks and then generate corresponding biometric data templates.
    • HR Manager Account Information: Basic information required to log in and use the App (provided by your employer/our Client).
  • Information Collected Automatically:
    • Device Information: We may collect standard device information from the HR Manager's device, necessary for app functionality and security.
    • Usage and Performance Data: We use tools like Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and aggregated usage data.
    • Advertising ID: The App requests access to the device's Advertising ID for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

  • To Register Employee Biometric Data
  • To Enable Biometric Verification
  • App Functionality and Security
  • App Improvement
  • Support
  • Compliance

Data Access and Sharing

Handling of information, particularly Employee biometric data, is strictly controlled:

  • Storage and Access: Employee photos and biometric data templates are stored securely on Clockin.it's central servers.
  • Availability within Clockin.it: The registered biometric data is made accessible through our secure platform to other authorized Clockin.it applications.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party service providers for functions like cloud hosting and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect the sensitive biometric information processed through this App against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Data Retention

Employee biometric data and photos are stored while the Employee is actively employed by the Client. After notification of dismissal or termination of access, this information will be permanently removed from our systems within 5 days.

Your Rights and Choices

HR Managers have rights regarding their account information and usage data. Employees have rights regarding their personal information, including biometric data. As the Client is the data controller, Employees should direct requests regarding their data primarily to their employer's HR department.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for the registration of individuals under the age of 16 or the relevant minimum age for employment. Clients are responsible for ensuring they have the necessary consents or legal basis for registering Employee data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify Clients of significant changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at policy@clockin.it. Employees should contact their employer's HR department first for inquiries about their data.
