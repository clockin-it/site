Information We Collect

In our clockin.it Single Demo application, we collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We collect location data to provide the functionality of our digital time clock service.
  • Device ID: We collect device IDs to ensure the proper functioning of our application on various devices.
  • Facial ID Data: We collect facial identification data for identity verification purposes.
  • Device Permissions: Access to the camera is used for capturing photos for facial recognition and clock-in records with photos. Exact location is used to log the clock-in location.

How We Use Your Information

The data collected is processed based on contract execution (full version) or user consent (demo version and specific features), as required by the LGPD. The collection of sensitive data, such as facial biometrics, is strictly based on the user's explicit consent.

The information we collect is used exclusively for demonstration purposes, including:

  • User identity verification;
  • Ensuring the proper functioning of our application;
  • Synchronizing data with our backend service before it is subsequently discarded.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. The data we collect is stored with encryption and securely shared with our backend service. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any collected data with third parties. Your information is used exclusively for the purposes stated in this policy.
